WIFI - A GREAT INVENTION

a very brief introduction of Wi-Fi

Nowadays, one of the most frequently spoken word is Wi-Fi. Whenever someone entering a restaurant or a cafe, the first thing that the person will be very likely to do is asking the password for the Wi-Fi. But what Wi-Fi is exactly?

The definition of Wi-Fi from Webopedia is: Wi-Fi is the name of a wireless networking technology that uses radio waves to provide wireless high-speed Internet and network connections.

Convenience brought by Wi-Fi:

  • Long distance communication
  • Easy access
  • Remote study and work
  • higher efficiency & productivity
  • etc.
